Residential

Brighten-up your favourite living room, increase your viewing area or create a stunning picture window over your garden - all through the effective use of fixed windows. These windows can also be coupled to your operable windows and doors or installed overhead as an economical way of welcoming additional natural light into any room. Whether square, rectangle or raked in shape, fixed windows add visual appeal and practicality to any home.
