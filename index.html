Moon & Baker, Aluminium Windows & Doors – A powerful Statement in residential Design.

Moon and Baker Windows & Doors are designed to improve aesthetics & tested to withstand the harsh weather. We at Moon & Baker understand Windows & Doors are responsible for allowing natural, light and ventilation into the home, maximising views and providing a level of security and privacy. It should also be remembered that windows and doors comprise around 25% of the average homes external appearance.

Moon & Baker (through Parent Company SSA-India) is the Exclusive Licensed manufacturer of G James Aluminium windows & Doors in India. G James was established in 1917 & is today one of Australia’s leading manufacturers of aluminium windows and doors, supplying to all sectors within the building and construction industry. G James 100 years of experience has been channelled into every aspect of our product range, customer service and industry knowledge as we strive to offer you the best value-for-money window and door in India.

So Welcome to our Website. The Moon & Baker Team Believes Home is not Just where the heart is. It’s where life happens. We hope to make that Home of yours as secure & beautiful as possible.
